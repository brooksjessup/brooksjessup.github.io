About Me

I'm a data scientist with a PhD and 10+ years of experience in higher education and academic research. I leverage my research experience to ground data science problems in real world contexts, and draw on my experience as an educator to effectively communicate analytical insights to non-technical audiences. I'm particularly interested in using big data in education to help students learn and instructors teach more effectively.

Course Recommender

Built an app to help jobseekers land their dream jobs by recommending relevant online courses. Collected data on 4,416 courses from Coursera’s public API and 3,324 jobs from Glassdoor. Built a machine learning model (Doc2Vec) to vectorize job descriptions and match them with course descriptions. Developed a Flask API to service users and deployed it to the web with Heroku.
